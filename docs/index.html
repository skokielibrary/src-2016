Summer Reading Skokie Public Library

Get ready for an awesome summer of reading, learning, and fun. Sign up at the library or on the bookmobile and get started earning your Friend Badge. From there, you can go anywhere your imagination takes you!

WHO WILL YOU BE?

Earn badges for trying new things. You can be an adventurer, a scientist, a musician, and more. We’ll drop new badges all summer long to keep your imagination working. Anyone with a Skokie Public Library card can participate—even adults!

WEAR YOUR PROGRESS

Get your camp lanyard and ID card when you sign up. After you earn your first badge, you’ll get an awesome t-shirt too! And for every badge you earn, you’ll have a cool button to show off.
