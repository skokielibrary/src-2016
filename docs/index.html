Summer Reading Skokie Public Library

Get ready for an awesome summer of reading, learning, and fun. Sign up at the library or on the bookmobile and get started earning your Adventure Badge. From there, you can go anywhere your imagination takes you!

WHERE WILL YOU GO?

Earn badges for trying new things and going new places. You can pretend to go to the moon, you can go on picnics, you can go back in time, and much more. Anyone with a Skokie Public Library card can participate—adults as well as kids!

WEAR YOUR PROGRESS

Get your lanyard and ID card when you sign up. After you earn your first badge, you’ll get an awesome t-shirt too! And for every badge you earn, you’ll have a cool button to show off.
